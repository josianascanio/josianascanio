## About Me

I'm a software developer focused on building practical business solutions with **Flutter**, **iDempiere ERP**, **POS workflows**, **REST APIs**, **AI agents**, **MCP servers**, and modern backend tools.

Currently, I work on mobile apps, ERP integrations, AI-powered automation, custom skills, business platforms, and tools that connect real workflows with intelligent agents.

## What I Do

- Build mobile apps with **Flutter** for Android and iOS
- Develop custom solutions for **iDempiere ERP**
- Create POS and business automation workflows
- Integrate systems using **REST APIs**, **Supabase**, and databases
- Work with **AI agents**, **OpenAI integrations**, **MCP**, and **OpenCode**
- Build custom **skills** and AI-assisted development workflows
- Create and manage platforms with **WordPress**, **Elementor**, and **Moodle**
- Deploy and configure services on **Linux**, **Nginx**, **Apache2**, and **Tomcat**
- Work with document management platforms like **Paperless** and **OpenKM**
- Publish and manage apps on **Google Play Store** and **Apple App Store**
